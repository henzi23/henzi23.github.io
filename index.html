Problem Statement

When someone decides to use bark mulch in their landscaping the first question that always comes up is "How much mulch do I need?"

This is a difficult question to answer for the layman as mulch comes in a cryptic volume based measure....cubic yards!!

  • Who knows what a cubic yard look likes?
  • How can one estimate how much area this covers?

What is the common home landscaper to do?

The Solution

Enter the Mulch Calculator App

We've created a simple app that allows the landscaping layman to accurately calculate the required cubic yards of mulch needed for their project.

All you need to know is:

  • the length and width of the area to be covered
  • the desired thickness of mulch to be laid (choose from 3 common thicknesses)

After entering the info the app does the number crunching and provided the predicted cubic yards of mulch required...just like magic.

Too easy you say....try for yourself!!

Link to Mulch Calculator App: https://henzi23.shinyapps.io/mulch/

How does it work?

I know you're thinking this is way too easy

  • How can one estimate this so quickly?
  • Is this just magic?
  • How accurate can the calculation be?

Well our team has harnessed the most accurate prediction calculation used by the professionals....the volume calculation!!

The secret is in the volume calculation below:

\[\text{cubic yards of mulch =} \frac{length(ft) * width(ft) * depth(in)}{324}\]

Example

  • Length = 30ft
  • Width = 20ft
  • Desired thickness is 3 inches

How much mulch do I need?

cubic_yard = round((30*20*3)/324,1)
cubic_yard

## [1] 5.6

You will require 5.6 cubic yards of bark mulch
